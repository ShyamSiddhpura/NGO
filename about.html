About Team Prayas

Our mission, vision, caretakers, and history of rescue operations in Surat.

Who We Are

A Sanctuary Built on Compassion

Team Prayas was born out of a desire to prevent the suffering of stray street animals in Surat. We operate a fully equipped open sanctuary aligned with green trees, where animals recover from severe injuries, trauma, or neglect. All rescue operations, ambulance dispatch, and surgeries are supervised by our qualified veterinary professionals.

Our Mission

To offer emergency first-aid trauma response, support safe spaying operations, and rehome healthy pets with verified adopters.

Our Vision

A urban community where stray cats and dogs live safely in harmony with residents, protected by mass Rabies vaccines.

Our Timeline

The Journey of Team Prayas

Trace our growth and achievements since our primary founding operations in Surat.

2018

NGO Founded

Started as a small group of rescue volunteers response teams feeding stray street dogs and rescuing injured kittens using personal transport.

2020

Helper Ambulance Launch

Acquired our first custom rescue ambulance vehicle, supporting a 24/7 medical trauma dispatch helpline for local residents in Surat.

2023

Surat Shelter Relocation

Relocated to our current green plots opposite the airport on Dumas Road. Built spacious open-air cages, quarantine units, and a vet clinic.

2026

5,000+ Spayed & Neutered

Milestone reached of performing safe Birth Control surgeries on over 5,000 stray cats and dogs, significantly improving urban health.
