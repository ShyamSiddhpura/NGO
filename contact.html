Frequently Asked Questions

Our process begins with filling out the online adoption registration form under the 'Adopt' page. Once submitted, our coordinator contacts you for a telephonic discussion, followed by a physical home check on your Surat residence. Once approved, you can visit the shelter on Dumas Road to pick up your companion.

No! Adoption is entirely free. However, we appreciate voluntary donations that directly go toward providing medical treatment for other injured strays inside our shelter.

All adult animals are sterilised (spayed for females, neutered for males) to prevent reproduction and improve biological health. All animals undergo a primary immunization course (7-in-1 vaccine for dogs, Tricat for cats, and Rabies vaccine) with detailed medical passport logs provided to adopters.
